What services does NS-Tech provide?

NS-Tech offers comprehensive digital transformation services including web development, custom software development, graphic design, SEO optimization, and social media management. We help businesses establish and enhance their digital presence.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and requirements. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take 6-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, and technical support. Our Professional and Enterprise packages include extended support periods, and we also offer monthly maintenance plans.

Can you help improve my website's search engine ranking?

Absolutely! Our SEO services include keyword research, on-page optimization, content strategy, and technical SEO improvements. We focus on sustainable, white-hat techniques to improve your search engine visibility and drive organic traffic.

What makes NS-Tech different from other digital agencies?

We combine technical expertise with creative design to deliver comprehensive digital solutions. Our team focuses on understanding your business goals and creating custom solutions that drive real results, backed by ongoing support and optimization.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes, we work with startups, small businesses, and large enterprises. Our scalable solutions and flexible packages are designed to meet the needs and budgets of businesses at any stage of growth.
